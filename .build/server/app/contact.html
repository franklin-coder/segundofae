Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create a custom piece?

Custom pieces typically take 2-3 weeks to complete, depending on complexity. We'll provide an estimated timeline when you place your order.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Shipping costs and times vary by location. Contact us for specific rates to your country.

What materials do you use in your jewelry?

We use high-quality, sustainable materials including cotton cord, natural beads, sterling silver findings, and ethically sourced stones.

Can I visit your studio in Victoria?

Absolutely! We love meeting customers in person. Please contact us to schedule an appointment, as we work by appointment only.

Still have questions?

Can't find what you're looking for? We're here to help! Send us a message and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
